### ROI Calculation Framework

```
Dame un framework completo para calcular ROI de marketing digital que sea comprensible y aplicable para empresas ecuatorianas, incluyendo costos, revenue attribution y timeframes.
```

**ROI Framework Ecuador:**

**Costos a incluir:**
- Staff time (horas × tarifa por hora)
- Tools y software subscriptions
- Advertising spend (Facebook Ads, LinkedIn Ads)
- Content creation costs (diseño, video, etc.)
- Training y education investments

**Revenue attribution:**
```
Direct Revenue:
- Sales con clara attribution a social media
- Upsells a clientes adquiridos via social
- Renewal rate improvement atribuible

Indirect Revenue:
- Brand awareness lift impact en sales offline
- Referrals generados por social proof
- Cost savings (menor dependencia en otros canales)
```

**Cálculo ROI específico:**
```
ROI = (Revenue Attributed - Investment) / Investment × 100

Ejemplo Ecuador:
Inversión mensual:
- Staff: $800 (community manager)
- Tools: $100 (software)
- Ads: $300 (publicidad)
- Content: $200 (diseño)
Total: $1,400

Revenue atribuido:
- Direct sales: $4,200
- Indirect impact: $1,800
Total: $6,000

ROI = ($6,000 - $1,400) / $1,400 = 329%
```

**Timeframes análisis:**
- ROI mensual: Tactical decisions
- ROI trimestral: Strategy adjustments
- ROI anual: Budget planning
- Customer LTV: Long-term planning
